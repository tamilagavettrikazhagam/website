A New Dawn for Tamil Nadu

Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam is a political party dedicated to the welfare and progress of the Tamil people. Founded by our thalapathy, the party is committed to addressing the pressing issues facing Tamil Nadu, including social justice, economic development, and cultural preservation.

Our Vision

We envision a Tamil Nadu that is prosperous, progressive, and socially just. A state where every individual, regardless of caste, creed, or religion, has equal opportunities.

Our Mission

  • Just: Ensuring equitable distribution of resources and opportunities.
  • Equitable: Promoting social justice and eliminating discrimination.
  • Prosperous: Driving economic growth and creating jobs.
  • Progressive: Embracing innovation and technology.
  • Culturally Rich: Preserving and promoting Tamil culture and heritage.

We are committed to social justice, economic equality, and the empowerment of the Tamil people. Our aim is to create a Tamil Nadu where every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential.
