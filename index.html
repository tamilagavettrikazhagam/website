TAMILAGA VETTRI KAZHAGAM

for Secular Social Justice

A New Dawn for Tamil Nadu

We are a political party dedicated to the welfare and progress of the Tamil people. Founded by our thalapathy, the party is committed to addressing the pressing issues facing Tamil Nadu, including social justice, economic development, and cultural preservation.

Tamil Desiyam & Dravidam: Our Ideals

Tamil Desiyam and Dravidam are the guiding principles of our party. While Tamil Desiyam fosters pride and unity among the Tamil people, Dravidam promotes social equality and justice. Together, they form the backbone of our mission, blending cultural pride with progressive values. By integrating both, we strive for a Tamil Nadu where every citizen thrives within a just and harmonious society.

Our Vision

We envision a Tamil Nadu that is prosperous, progressive, and socially just. A state where every individual, regardless of caste, creed, or religion, has equal opportunities.

Our Mission

Just Ensuring equitable distribution of resources and opportunities.
Equitable Promoting social justice and eliminating discrimination.
Prosperous Driving economic growth and creating jobs.
Progressive Embracing innovation and technology.
Culturally Rich Preserving and promoting Tamil culture and heritage.

"We are committed to social justice, economic equality, and the empowerment of the Tamil people. Our aim is to create a Tamil Nadu where every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential."
